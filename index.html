Game Introduction

Long, boring text:
Simple introduction into our game. Every Player starts with a dragon egg. InGame you will be able to breed your dragon, to start your journey. Also, you will be given one small island, on which you can build various stuff. You can build defensive towers, traps, buildings, utilitiy buildings and more. Clans have an island, that they will share. Every clanmember, if the rights have been given, can interact with the island. They can use special clan perks, such as training boosts of troops, better breeding potions and more. As a dragon, you can recruit small Gublins. There are multiple Gublins that you can use, to attack any island! If you've attacked the island, you can steal items, 15% of the tokens (that you need to savely store in your shelter) and more. You can conquer Islands by a new modus, if you find somebody who is willing to fight you. The winner gets the Island with tokens, the loser will just get some tokens. Islands are also purchaseable in the store. But watch out! You can always be attacked..
